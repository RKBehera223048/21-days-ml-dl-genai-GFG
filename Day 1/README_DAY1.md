## 🔑 Key Findings
- **Gender Impact:** Women had ~74% survival rate vs ~19% for men
- **Class Matters:** 1st class had ~63% survival vs ~24% for 3rd class
- **Age Factor:** Children had higher survival rates
- **Family Size:** Small families (2-4) had better survival rates
- **Fare Correlation:** Higher fare correlated with better survival
